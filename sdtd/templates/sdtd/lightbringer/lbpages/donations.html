Donations

All donations are greatly appreciated!

Why do we ask for donations?
Well, the servers that you play on are owned and ran by players as a courtesy for other players. These servers, and sometimes the licensing with it (for 3rd Party applications), is paid for and maintained by the Admins. Some players are considerate enough to help make sure a server is funded and will be running for at least the next couple months. Any donation made to Alliance-Gaming are used directly for the servers and nothing else.
Donation Info
Be sure to put your game name and server in the Paypal ‘purpose’ field to ensure proper benefits.
Donation Perks: Players will need to specify which server they wish to recieve perks on.
Perks are Server Specific. If you donate to one sever, but decide to switch to another, you must donate to that server to recieve its perks.
Previous donations DO NOT stack with future donations, unless otherwise stated by the Admin or in a game message.
All transactions are individual and final.
PLEASE NOTE THAT PERKS ARE NOT INSTANT!!! AN ADMIN MUST LOG ON IN ORDER TO GRANT PERKS. ADMINS ARE ON DAILY AND HAVE ALERTS FOR THE GAME. WE WILL DO OUR BEST TO PROVIDED TO APPROPRIATE PERKS AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. THANK YOU FOR YOUR UNDERSTANDING AND PATIENCE. FOR DONATION INQUIRIES OR PROBLEMS, PLEASE EMAIL US AT ALLIANCE.GAMING.MANAGER@GMAIL.COM
Disclaimer
Thank you for your consideration to donate! Note that donating does not mean you are now above rules, admins, other players, or general manners.
You are donating to ensure the continued support of this server and because you enjoy the perks, benefits and general gameplay, and community of the server.
This does not mean you will decide how the server is ran or who is banned/allowed.
Breaking rules can and will result in Kicks/Bans despite donation amount or status.

SDP = Single Donation Perks, will be given on first donation, OPD will be given starting the 1st recurring payment
Players may combine a Single donation perk with a monthly per to obtain a higher SDP.
For example: a player amy donater $30 then within 24 hours start a $20 monthly sub, for a total single payment of $50. The player will be given the Super Donor SPD, and on each following monthly payment of $20 the player will recieved the Tier IV OPD perks
